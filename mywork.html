Game Development Project

Managed to launch a journey-based online Spin & Win campaign with an incentive to promote email signup by Salesforce Marketing Cloud. From idea to implementation, the project involved building delivery plans, trigger journeys, content, designing data flow, UX design of the game, sign-up procedure and conducting user acceptance test. To drive email acquisition, massive marketing communications have been launched through multiple touchpoints through different channels online and offline. As a result, new signup increased by 260% while sales increased by 50% during the campaign period YoY in 2020; the Spin & Win campaign became the best-performing campaign between 2020 and 2021.
